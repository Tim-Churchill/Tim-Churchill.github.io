Drone Path Planning
and Control

Work with NAPP Lab on path planning and control for Crazyflie drones.

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing planning and control methods for small quadrotor drones. The goal is to reliably move a Crazyflie platform through complex environments while maintaining stability and repeatable trajectories.

Focus Areas

  • Trajectory generation with safety and feasibility constraints.
  • Robust control loops that maintain stability in real-world conditions.
  • Integration with motion capture feedback for precise state estimation.
